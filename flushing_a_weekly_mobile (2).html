Flushing A Diner – 4-Week Positivity Plan

Week 1: Welcome Back, Flushing!

+

Theme: Community connection & nostalgia

In-Restaurant:

  • Flashback Thursday – retro music, throwback prices on classic burgers
  • Bring a Friend Special – Buy 1 meal, get the second half off

Social Media:

  • Post old diner photos: “Where Flushing comes together since [year]!”
  • Poll: “Which classic burger should return for a limited-time special?”

Community Engagement:

  • Invite local schools to submit art for a mini gallery displayed in the diner

Week 2: Seasonal Specials Kickoff

+

Theme: Fall flavors & seasonal excitement

In-Restaurant:

  • Pumpkin Spice Milkshake, Autumn Chili Dog, Maple Bacon Burger
  • Taste Test Tuesday – guests vote on which seasonal item becomes permanent

Social Media:

  • Short TikTok/Reels of milkshake prep: “Fall in a cup!”
  • Customer testimonial or staff spotlight highlighting new items

Community Engagement:

  • Partner with local pumpkin farm – mini pumpkin giveaway or visit photos

Week 3: Family & Fun Week

+

Theme: Accessible dining & community warmth

In-Restaurant:

  • Family Pack Meals (2 burgers + 2 hot dogs + 2 milkshakes)
  • Kids’ Creativity Hour – coloring sheets or cupcake decorating

Social Media:

  • Contest: “Best family diner photo this week!” Winner gets free dessert
  • Share staff stories about favorite menu items growing up

Community Engagement:

  • Canned goods donation – discount per item: “Flushing A Cares!”

Week 4: Community Celebration

+

Theme: Positive local energy & engagement

In-Restaurant:

  • Live Music Friday – local bands or solo performers
  • Flushing Pride Day – free cookie with any meal

Social Media:

  • Video reel of events highlighting smiles and fun
  • Thank-you message celebrating loyal customers and staff

Community Engagement:

  • Collaborate with a local business for cross-promotions

Ongoing Daily/Weekly Actions

+
  • Staff Spotlights: post one team member per week with fun facts
  • Positive Quotes or Fun Facts: share uplifting content about Flushing or diner culture
  • Customer Engagement: encourage photos with #FlushingAFun hashtag

Optional Mini Campaigns

+
  • Secret Menu Friday: reveal hidden item; first 20 customers get it free
  • Local Heroes Night: discount for teachers, firefighters, police
  • Interactive Instagram Stories: polls like “Choose next week’s milkshake flavor”
